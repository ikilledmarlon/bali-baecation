Private Water Purification — Tri Desna

📍 Tri Desna · Sayan, Ubud · Private Melukat Ceremony

This isn't a tourist temple line — it's a private ceremony just for us. It starts with a reading session with Ibu Desak, then a sacred water purification (Melukat) with holy floral water, and ends with a blessing ceremony with Ibu Helena. About 1–1.5 hours. They provide everything — sarong, offerings, towel.

⚡ Private ceremony — no other guests during our session

Rice Terraces at Dawn

📍 Tegallalang Rice Terrace · 20 min north of Ubud · UNESCO World Heritage

Layered rice paddies carved into the hills, part of Bali's traditional Subak irrigation system. Especially magical at dawn when the light is soft and crowds are minimal. Bring small rupiah notes — many collection points don't give change.

⚡ Arrive before 8AM — paths open ~6AM, ticket booths open ~8AM

Nusa Lembongan — Island Escape

📍 Nusa Lembongan · Smaller & more relaxed than Bali · Turquoise water, mangroves, manta rays

One of Bali's most beloved island getaways — turquoise waters that feel unreal, coral gardens, manta ray snorkeling, beachfront chill spots, and an easygoing island pace. More developed than Nusa Ceningan/Penida but way more chill than mainland Bali.

🏖️ Where to Stay
Jungbatu Beach — Long white sand, cafes, bars, sunset views. Best for nightlife, casual dining, dive shops.
Mushroom Bay — Calmer, more boutique. Curved bay with reef protection, gentler waves. Best for couples, chill brunches, relaxed swims.
📍 Spots to Hit
🍹 Savaya Bali — Luxury oceanfront lounge · Pool + daybeds · Sunset DJ sets · Med-tropical food
Daybed min spend: ~500K–1.5M+ · Cocktails: ~120K–220K · Small plates: ~90K–170K
🌿 Mangrove Tour — Kayak through canals, birdwatching, hidden lagoons. Great mid-day break from sun.
❌ Lago by Pontoon — Temporarily closed · @lago.bali for updates
🗺️ 3–4 Day Itinerary
Day 1 — Arrival: Check in at Jungbatu or Mushroom Bay → sunset beach walk → dinner beachfront
Day 2 — Water Day: Morning snorkel/manta tour → lunch at Savaya → mangrove tour → sunset swim + cocktails
Day 3 — Dive Adventure: Half or full day diving → sunset at Mushroom Bay → relaxed dinner
Day 4 — Depart: Beach yoga → brunch → fast boat to Bali or next island

Uluwatu — The Grand Finale

Clifftop temples, a fire dance at sunset, and our final reflection dinner overlooking the Indian Ocean — right near the airport for our flight out.

🌅

Sunset at the Cliff Temple

📍 Uluwatu Temple (Pura Luhur Uluwatu) · 70m above the Indian Ocean

An 11th-century sea temple on a sheer limestone cliff — one of Bali's six spiritual pillars. At sunset, the cliffs glow gold, waves crash below, and the sky turns surreal shades of orange and violet. This is less of a temple visit and more of a cinematic moment. Arrive 4:30–5:00 PM.

💰 Costs
Temple entrance: 50K
Kecak Fire Dance: 150K/person · ~6PM · 60 min · Cliffside amphitheater
Parking: 5K–10K
Sarong: Usually included
Bring small cash
🐒 Monkey Warning
Uluwatu's monkeys are smart. They will grab sunglasses, snatch phones, pull hair, and open bags.
✔ No loose accessories ✔ Phone secure ✔ Don't feed them ✔ Stay calm
Staff can retrieve stolen items (sometimes with fruit bribes)
  • Arrive 4:30–5PM for sunset
  • Watch Kecak Fire Dance (~150K)
  • Secure phones & sunglasses from monkeys
  • Final reflection dinner together
