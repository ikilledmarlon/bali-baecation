Nyepi — Bali's Silent Day

📍 March 19–20 · 24 Hours · Bali Shuts Down Completely

Nyepi is Bali's Day of Silence. For 24 hours: no flights, no transport, no movement, no lights. The entire island goes dark and silent. It's sacred and non-negotiable.

⚡ We'll be on Gili Air — NOT affected by Nyepi shutdown
🛑 Bali During Nyepi
No flights in/out · No transport · No movement outside · No lights after dark
⚠️ Do NOT schedule ferry departing Bali on March 19
✅ Gili Air / Lombok During Nyepi
NOT fully shut down · Restaurants mostly operate · Calm, peaceful day
Best move: Stay on Gili Air through Nyepi
🔓 March 20 (After 6AM) — Return Options
Mar 20: Morning from Gili Air → Bangsal → Lembar → midday ferry → evening back in Bali

🐢 Option D — Slow Route via Lombok (Sea-Sick Safe)
Longer but smoother — large government ferry + short final hop. Best if seasickness is severe.
Leg 1: Gili Air → Bangsal (Lombok)
Public boat · 10–15 min · ~15K–30K (~$1–2) · Leaves every 30–60 min
Arrives at Bangsal Harbor · Buy ticket at counter
Leg 2: Bangsal → Lembar Harbor
Private car/taxi · 1.5–2 hrs · ~375K–600K (~$25–40)
Drive across Lombok to reach the ferry port on the west coast
Leg 3: Lembar → Padang Bai (Bali)
ASDP government ferry · 4–5 hrs · ~75K–150K (~$5–10) · Large stable roll-on/roll-off
Book at trip.ferizy.com or buy at port · Runs every 1–2 hrs
Leg 4: Padang Bai → Nusa Lembongan
Fast boat · ~45 min · ~375K–600K (~$25–40)
Short fast-boat hop — usually calmer waters than open sea crossings
Book at gilibookings.com or ekajayafastferry.com
⏱ Total: 7–9 hours · ~$65–100 · Leave early morning
Consider splitting into 2 days: Day 1 to Padang Bai (sleep in East Bali), Day 2 to Lembongan

Nusa Lembongan — Island Escape

📍 Nusa Lembongan · Smaller & more relaxed than Bali · Turquoise water, mangroves, manta rays

One of Bali's most beloved island getaways — turquoise waters that feel unreal, coral gardens, manta ray snorkeling, beachfront chill spots, and an easygoing island pace. More developed than Nusa Ceningan/Penida but way more chill than mainland Bali.

🏖️ Where to Stay
Jungbatu Beach — Long white sand, cafes, bars, sunset views. Best for nightlife, casual dining, dive shops.
Mushroom Bay — Calmer, more boutique. Curved bay with reef protection, gentler waves. Best for couples, chill brunches, relaxed swims.
📍 Spots to Hit
🍹 Savaya Bali — Luxury oceanfront lounge · Pool + daybeds · Sunset DJ sets · Med-tropical food
Daybed min spend: ~500K–1.5M+ · Cocktails: ~120K–220K · Small plates: ~90K–170K
🌿 Mangrove Tour — Kayak through canals, birdwatching, hidden lagoons. Great mid-day break from sun.
❌ Lago by Pontoon — Temporarily closed · @lago.bali for updates
🗺️ 3–4 Day Itinerary
Day 1 — Arrival: Check in at Jungbatu or Mushroom Bay → sunset beach walk → dinner beachfront
Day 2 — Water Day: Morning snorkel/manta tour → lunch at Savaya → mangrove tour → sunset swim + cocktails
Day 3 — Dive Adventure: Half or full day diving → sunset at Mushroom Bay → relaxed dinner
Day 4 — Depart: Beach yoga → brunch → fast boat to Bali or next island
